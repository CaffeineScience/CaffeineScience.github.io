Chemical Properties

  1. pH of 6.9
  2. Soluble in Water
  3. Flammable
  4. When Heated to Decomposition, Caffeine Emits Toxic Fumes of Nitrogen Oxides
  5. Half-Life of 3-5 Hours in Adults

Physical Properties

  1. White Powder or White Glistening Needles
  2. Odorless
  3. Bitter Taste
  4. Boils at 178℃
  5. Density of 1.23g/cm³
